# AIDX Singapore Positioning Design

## Goal

Make AIDX's Singapore identity immediately scannable on the homepage without
changing the existing project-card structure or overstating the designer's role.

## Approved Direction

Use strong, two-level positioning:

- Change the project-kind label from a generic live-website label to
  `Singapore AI company` in English and `新加坡 AI 公司` in Chinese.
- Begin the proposition by identifying AIDX as a Singapore AI safety company,
  then retain the existing explanation of the website work across interface,
  information structure, and motion.

## Copy

English:

- Kind: `Singapore AI company`
- Proposition: `A live website for AIDX, a Singapore AI safety company, shaped through interface, information structure, and motion.`

Chinese:

- Kind: `新加坡 AI 公司`
- Proposition: `为新加坡 AI 安全公司 AIDX 打造的线上官网，通过界面、信息结构与动效塑造品牌表达。`

## Scope

Only update the AIDX localized `kind` and `proposition` strings. Preserve the
card layout, status, role/scope, media, caption, action, destination, and all
other homepage modules.

## Verification

- English and Chinese component renders contain the approved Singapore labels
  and propositions.
- The AIDX external destination and safe new-tab attributes remain unchanged.
- Existing homepage tests and content validation pass.
