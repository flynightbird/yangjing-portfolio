# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- Senior design hiring managers evaluating Yang Jing for senior product design roles.
- Product leaders and founders seeking end-to-end product design and AI-assisted prototyping for selected freelance engagements.
- Design peers assessing interaction craft, systems thinking, and delivery quality.

## Product Purpose

The portfolio supports job opportunities and freelance engagements with equal weight: 50% job search and 50% freelance. It must show one coherent professional identity across consumer-scale product design, AI and B2B system complexity, deep interaction design, and AI-assisted building.

## Brand Personality

Precise, technical, human.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI or futuristic decoration.
- Purple gradients.
- Glassmorphism.
- Beige portfolio templates.
- Marketing-style card grids.
- Copies of the Adham Dannaway or Jonny Czar layouts. Their work may inform specific principles, but this portfolio must use an original visual and interaction system.
- Fabricated metrics or testimonials.

## Design Principles

1. Make the dual Product Designer and AI-native Builder role immediately legible.
2. Put evidence before decoration.
3. Present one coherent professional identity rather than two unrelated careers.
4. Concentrate motion in selective, high-value moments.
5. Use real product media and maintain truthful disclosure and evidence boundaries.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Meet WCAG 2.2 AA targets.
- Provide reduced-motion alternatives.
- Maintain keyboard parity for every click interaction.
- Use visible focus states.
- Size touch targets for mobile use.
- Keep essential content available without hover.
